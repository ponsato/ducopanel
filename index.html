wDUCO wrap

Duino-Coin is a hybrid currency, meaning that it can be converted to wDUCO - Duino wrapped on the Tron network (as a decentralized token). You can read more about it here

Please note that this feature is currently in beta and may not work fully yet. For better stability and unwrap functions please use our CLI Wallet.
Warning: your Tron wallet needs to support tokens, otherwise wrapped DUCO may get lost.
